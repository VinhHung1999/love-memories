# Creative Brief: Love Memories Commercialization

**Date**: March 2026
**Prepared by**: CMO Analysis
**Status**: Draft for Founder Review

---

## Executive Summary

Love Memories is positioned to enter a $2B global couples app market (growing 12-15% CAGR) with a **differentiated "full-stack couples OS" positioning** that no competitor currently occupies. The app's 14-module breadth -- spanning memories, food spots, recipes, goals, love letters, expenses, and more -- creates a unique value proposition against fragmented competitors who each own only 1-2 verticals.

**The core insight**: Couples today scatter their relationship across 5-6 separate apps. Love Memories is the consolidation play.

**Recommended strategy**: Vietnam-first launch with freemium pricing ($1.99/mo VND-equivalent), targeting 10,000-25,000 couple signups in Year 1, with expansion to Vietnamese diaspora and broader SEA by end of Year 1.

**Expected Year-1 revenue range**: $5,400-$27,500 (marketing efficiency scenarios -- CFO input needed for full P&L).

**Required investment**: ~$15,000-$20,000 marketing budget + development time for subscription infrastructure.

## 3. Positioning & Brand

### Positioning Statement

```
FOR couples (18-35) tired of scattering their relationship across 6 apps
WHO want one private space to capture memories, plan dates, track goals, and manage life together
LOVE MEMORIES is an all-in-one couples companion app
THAT replaces Google Photos + Splitwise + Notes + Maps + Trello with one beautiful, private experience
UNLIKE Between (messaging-centric), Paired (therapy-centric), or Honeydue (finance-only)
WE provide the only 14-module couples operating system that grows more valuable with every memory you add
```

### Tagline

**Primary**: "Your relationship, one app."

### Brand Personality

**The Companion** -- warm, thoughtful, reliable, playful, private. Not a therapist (Paired), not an entertainer (Lovewick), not a utility (Honeydue). A trusted friend that helps couples capture and celebrate their life together.

### Visual Identity

- **Current palette (KEEP)**: Rose `#E8788A` + Apricot `#F4A261` + Mint `#7EC8B5` -- warm, romantic, differentiated
- **Typography (KEEP)**: Playfair Display (headings) + Inter (body) -- premium "personal journal" feel
- **Add**: Dark mode, premium gold accent (`#D4A574`), warm white backgrounds (`#FFF8F5`)

5. **Influencer partnerships** -- 2-3 Vietnamese lifestyle creators

## 10. Confidence Assessment

**Overall Confidence**: MEDIUM

**What we know (HIGH confidence)**:
- The couples app market is real and growing ($2B, 12-15% CAGR)
- No dominant Vietnamese-native couples app exists
- Love Memories has genuinely unique feature breadth (14 modules)
- The food/dining vertical is completely uncontested

**What we assume (MEDIUM confidence)**:
- Vietnamese couples aged 22-35 will pay $1.99/mo for a couples app
- A 14-module app can be positioned as "comprehensive" rather than "overwhelming"
- ASO + TikTok + Instagram can drive 15K-25K installs at <$0.50 CPD in Vietnam

**What we don't know (LOW confidence, needs research)**:
- [ ] Actual willingness-to-pay for Vietnamese couples (need: survey of 200+ couples)
- [ ] Which 3 features drive the most retention (need: analytics after launch)
- [ ] Competitive response from Between (Krafton-backed) or Paired in VN market
- [ ] Whether "full-stack couples OS" positioning resonates or overwhelms (need: landing page A/B test)

**Bias check performed**:
- Survivorship bias: Acknowledged that successful couples apps (Between, Paired) had years and millions in funding. Love Memories' path will differ.
- Confirmation bias: Actively sought competitor strengths (Paired's therapy depth, Between's 12-year data moat) rather than only cataloging weaknesses.
- Recency bias: Market size estimates come from multiple 2024-2025 sources, not extrapolated from older data.
